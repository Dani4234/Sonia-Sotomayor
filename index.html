Sonia Sotomayor

My name is Sonia Sotomayor and I am running for the upcomming 2020 Presidential Elections. I would like to have your vote because I belive that this country should have a better educational system and better wages for everyone regardless of their political status.In addition I believe that every one should have the chance to go to college and pursue a career. I want to fix the educational structure our country has and give the children the support and advancement they need. Our country needs someone who will care about their needs and give importance to every problem in order to achieve a better place and community for everyone to live in and participate in.

  • Served in the U.S court of Appeals for the Second Circuit since October 1998.
  • Born to a Puerto Rican family
  • Sotomayor graduated as valedictorian of her class at Blessed Sacrament and at Cardinal Spellman High School in New York.
  • At Princeton, she continued to excel, graduating summa cum laude, and Phi Beta Kappa. She was a co-recipient of the M. Taylor Pyne Prize, the highest honor Princeton awards to an undergraduate.
